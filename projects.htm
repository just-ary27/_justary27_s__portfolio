Jane-ify! The Discord Bot

Made during the Makers 2021, this bot is more powerful than you think! From playing music to solving math problems, there's probably much more than what a single bot on your server can do. Jane-ify was made for replacing all the bots on your server. It can also talk to you, greet you according to time, do moderation tasks like kicking or banning a member. If you are confused then you can always type help! Jane-ify is learning new things every now & then!
